Capabilities

What NeoBrowser Does

dark_mode

Real Chrome, Zero Setup

A headless Chrome launches per MCP process with your cookies and localStorage already synced. Sites where you're logged in just work.

bolt
Ghost Chrome — isolated profile, real session
sync

Your Browser, Their Session

Cookies, localStorage, and IndexedDB from your real Chrome profile. Google domains excluded to protect your login.

fork_right

HTTP Fast Path + Chrome CDP

Static pages in 0.11s via HTTP. SPAs and Cloudflare pages in 1s via Chrome DevTools Protocol. Best of both worlds.

construction

Navigate, Click, Fill, Extract

browse, search, open, read, find, click, type, fill, submit, scroll, wait, login, extract, screenshot, js, gpt, grok, plugin, status.

smart_toy

Talk to ChatGPT and Grok

Dedicated persistent tabs. Send messages, read responses, check streaming status, get conversation history.

draft

One File. One Dependency.

neo-browser.py — 1500 lines of Python. Only needs the websockets package. No Selenium, no Playwright, no chromedriver.
